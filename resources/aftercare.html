Long-Term Maintenance (3-5 Years)

  • Moisturize scalp regularly (keeps skin healthy)
  • Gentle exfoliation 1-2x/week after full healing
  • Avoid harsh chemicals and abrasive scrubs
  • Schedule touch-ups every 3-5 years ($250/hr at Stella's)
  • Sun protection always (UV breaks down pigment faster)

Common Aftercare Mistakes (Don't Do These!)

  1. Getting scalp wet in Days 1-4: Ruins pigment retention
  2. Sweating heavily too soon: Causes pigment to lift
  3. Picking at scabs: Creates patchy, uneven results
  4. Shaving too early: Can damage healing pigment
  5. Skipping sun protection: Accelerates fading
  6. Using harsh products: Irritates healing skin
  7. Swimming within 10 days: Chlorine/salt damage pigment

Recommended Products

Shampoo (Days 5+)

  • Gentle, sulfate-free, natural formulas
  • Baby shampoo (mild, non-irritating)
  • Avoid: Harsh clarifying shampoos, dandruff treatments

Sunscreen (Day 10+)

  • Broad-spectrum SPF 30 or higher
  • Physical (mineral) sunscreens preferred (zinc oxide, titanium dioxide)
  • Reapply every 2 hours when outdoors

Moisturizer (Optional, after healing)

  • Non-comedogenic, fragrance-free lotions
  • Keeps scalp hydrated and healthy

FAQ: Aftercare Questions

What if I accidentally get my scalp wet in Days 1-4?

Pat dry immediately with a clean towel. Light splashing (quick rinse) is less damaging than prolonged exposure. Inform your practitioner at your next session‚Äîyou may need additional pigment in that area.

Can I wear a hat after SMP?

Yes, but avoid tight or rubbing hats in the first 4 days. Loose-fitting hats for sun protection are fine. After Day 5, normal hats are okay.

Is scabbing normal?

Yes. Light scabbing is part of the healing process. Do not pick or scratch! Let scabs fall off naturally‚Äîpicking causes patchy results.
